Short Introduction

French is spoken in France, Belgium, Canada, and many African countries. It is known as the language of culture, art, and diplomacy.

Basic Phrases

  • Bonjour — Hello
  • Ça va ? — How are you?
  • Merci — Thank you
  • Je m’appelle … — My name is …
  • Tu viens d’où ? — Where are you from?
