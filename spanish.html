Short Introduction

Spanish is spoken in Spain, most Latin American countries, and parts of the United States. It is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world.

Basic Phrases

  • Hola — Hello
  • ¿Cómo estás? — How are you?
  • Gracias — Thank you
  • Me llamo … — My name is …
  • ¿De dónde eres? — Where are you from?
  • Buenos días — Good morning
  • Buenas noches — Good night
