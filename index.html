🇩🇪 Deutsch (German)

Short Introduction

German is spoken in Germany, Austria, and parts of Switzerland.

Basic Phrases

  • Hallo — Hello
  • Wie geht’s? — How are you?
  • Danke — Thank you
  • Ich heiße … — My name is …
  • Woher kommst du? — Where are you from?

Important Vocabulary
